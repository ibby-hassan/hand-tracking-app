This system utilises your front camera to allow the control of a cursor touchlessly using the motion of your hand.

You will see video feedback in the background. Hold your hand to the camera until the identifies it and moves to your location.
Holding a fist will register clicks.

For best results, use slow motions and keep an OPEN hand. Stand a step back from the device. Hold your arm outstreches and palm facing the camera. Keep your other hand down.
